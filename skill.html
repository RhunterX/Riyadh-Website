What is PSSCM?

PSSCM is a Silat association recognized in Malaysia. This association has been long established in Malaya and was used in the Kedah Sultanate Palace in the past. This Silat was once forgotten but was succesfully reborn and has survived until now. Alhamdulilah, I am one of the people in the world who owns this precious heritage of Malay People.
